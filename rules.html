Breakout {Gravity} - Rules

Play Game

Destroy all the bricks. More bricks destroyed, more coins earned. This is your goal. After all, it's a breakout game.

Press space to start the game. The ball will start to fall.

Use left and right arrows to control the paddle's direction. Press start to hit the ball. The «moment» you hit the ball will determine it's speed. Use down arrow to toggle between a normal size and a small size paddle.
